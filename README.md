## Research Projects

### 3. Depression Coping Mechanisms
**Focus**: Evidence-based strategies for managing depression, including non-medication approaches, therapy options, lifestyle modifications, and modern treatment research.

**Key Topics**:
- Understanding depression from various sources (family issues, work stress, unemployment, fatigue)
- Psychotherapy approaches (CBT, IPT, ACT)
- Lifestyle modifications (exercise, sleep, nutrition, mindfulness)
- Medication options with pros and cons
- Alternative therapies (light therapy, TMS, supplements)
- Practical tools and daily coping strategies
- Work-specific and family-specific management approaches
- Long-term maintenance and relapse prevention
