## Research Topics

### [Streaming Strategies Research](./StreamingStrategiesResearch/)
Comprehensive research on profitable streaming strategies, platform analysis, audience growth techniques, content optimization, monetization methods, and specialized guidance for game development streaming with MonoGame.

This research covers:
- Platform analysis and comparison (Twitch, YouTube, Kick, Facebook Gaming, TikTok)
- Competitive analysis of streaming subjects and growth patterns
- Audience growth strategies and proven techniques
- Content format analysis (shorts, long-form, hybrid approaches)
- Monetization strategies and funding methods
- Best practices for successful streamers
- Game development streaming specialization
- MonoGame-specific streaming tips and competitive positioning
